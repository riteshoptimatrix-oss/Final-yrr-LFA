Project Overview

Lead Finder AI Automation is a production-grade, AI-powered lead generation and CRM automation platform designed for modern business intelligence and sales workflows. The platform automatically discovers, scrapes, organizes, analyzes, and manages business leads from multiple online sources.

The system uses a geographic hierarchy — State → City → Area — to enable hyperlocal lead discovery, making it the ideal tool for sales teams operating in specific regions or localities.

🌐

Data Sources

  • Google Maps
  • JustDial
  • IndiaMart
🎯

Target Audience

  • Lead generation agencies
  • Sales teams
  • Marketing companies
  • CRM operations
  • Business intelligence workflows
02

The main objective is to automate hyperlocal business lead generation with real-time CRM management and analytics. The platform provides a unified ecosystem that bridges the gap between raw data discovery and actionable sales intelligence.

06

Search & Scraping System

The user inputs a Business Type, State, City, Area and selects sources. Example: Salon · Gujarat · Ahmedabad · Naroda. The Playwright scraping engine then processes this through a multi-step validation pipeline.

Search Request → Scraper Engine → Playwright Browser → Source Extraction → Data Validation → Duplicate Removal → MongoDB Storage → Frontend Update

Extracted Business Data Fields

🏢 Business Name 🌐 Website 📞 Phone Number 📍 Address ⭐ Rating 💬 Reviews 🏷️ Business Category 📡 Source Platform

09

Automation System

The Automation Engine automates area-wise business scraping across multiple categories simultaneously. It supports resume capability — if automation stops mid-process, it resumes from the last completed position, preserving all previously completed jobs.

State → City → Area → Business Type → Multi-Source Scraping → MongoDB Storage → CRM Sync
🏷️

Multi-Business Automation

  • Salon, Gym, Grocery Store
  • Auto-creates scraping jobs per category
  • Per area combination support
♻️

Resume Automation

  • Resume from last position
  • Continue pending jobs
  • Preserve completed jobs

Area Heatmap

The interactive heatmap visualizes business density geographically across areas. Dynamic markers with area tooltips allow users to click and filter leads by region instantly. Example: Satellite → 420 Businesses · Prahlad Nagar → 310 · Naroda → 120

🟢 Green
Low Density — Few businesses in this area
🟡 Yellow
Medium Density — Moderate business presence
🔴 Red
High Density — Saturated business zone
